5.49 Using the Segmentation Tagger

The Alembic Workbench Segmentation Tagger is designed to help you quickly segment or re-segment (add, remove, and adjust spaces in) a document. There are two major modes of the Segmentation Tagger, "Segment" and "Edit", which are toggled by buttons in the upper right hand corner of the interface. When in "Edit" mode, the tagger behaves as a standard text editor. When in "Segment" mode, standard text editing is disabled, and a few special mouse bindings are available for quick segmentation:
Button 1: insert a space character
Button 2 (drag): move a space character
Button 3: delete a space character
